Phenomena

Regions are unique for their vast geographical size, large population, immense connectivity, and variation in scale. While this set of conditions is typical of regions across the world, how they manifest can have tremendous variation. These Regional Phenomena help to understand how regions function, and in turn, how to influence their function.
